About The Project

This project aims to collect how people view images and how their vission differs between people who have 20/20 vision and people who have visual impairments. This can be just colour blindness, astigmatism or more serious impairments. Once you click lets begin below, you will be taken to a login/create user page this will allow you to come back and continue at any point via logging in.

The Process

  • Create an account, this will allow you to continue the data collection at any point.
  • Answer a small survey about your vision.
  • If you wear contacts or glasses please remove them before looking at the images
  • Look at as many images as you like in the dataset, following where you look on the image with your mouse cursor. You can close at any point and continue where you left off when you return.
